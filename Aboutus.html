ABHYUDAYA-The Social Arm of JECRC

Abhyudaya is a collective word for the social verticals of JECRC FOUNDATION. It is bifurcated into 4 clauses comprising : Aashiyan,Zarurat,Suhasini and Soch under its wings.

Abhyuday, was founded in _,It was started with an initial strength of _ volunteers having a zeal and zest to augment a body, which can provide a window to the students of the institute who wish to work for the betterment of mankind. It Started with a clear cut vision to channelize the fledgling energy of the youth towards a virtuous cause.

VISION

To saw the seeds of solicitude and togetherness which in future are expected to develop an sanguine approach so that the mankind infuse their faith in virtue.

MISSION

To prepare less privileged children of India through holistic support to succeed in a rapidly changing environment.

ZARURAT

↻

Zarurat is a student-led social club of JECRC University founded in 2011. Our main motto is to provide the elementary education to the children who are incapable to receive it. Zarurat is the path for children’s well being or providing the wealth of education to the needy ones. Recently we are providing education to the children whi are living in environs of JECRC University, Jaipur

SOCH

↻

Soch is a student hold social club organised by students of JECRC University. It was established in 2014, with multi many mottos which includes the events like Cleanliness Drives, CPR training, Vastra Samman, Orphanage – Old age home visits. Being a multi-purpose club we focuses on every aspect for the well being of society.

AASHAYEIN

↻

The foundation of Aashayein was led in the year of 2006 by a group of geeks of JECRC. It is a social initiative with a motto to meet the demand of blood shortage. It is one of the social columns under the umberella of Abhyudaya which works 24x7. We are available for for 5 everyone who needs blood donation and we try our best to reach ones need. Little contribution can help a lot.

SUHASNI

↻

The word Suhasini means Ever smiling. One of the vertical columns under the umberella of abhyudaya is Suhasini with the motto of “Saving and spreading smile of a Girl Child.
Suhasini is extremely determined in uplifting the education of girl child. For the same, Suhasini has adopted more than 5 villages and is also monitoring 12+ Government schools. Having a healthy engagement of volunteers and mentors the social column has inspired more than 220 girls to go to school.
